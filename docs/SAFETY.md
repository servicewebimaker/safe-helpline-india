# Safety

Safe Helpline India is an information project. It is not an emergency service, responder network, government service, or crisis counseling service.

## Emergency Disclaimer

In immediate danger, use the phone dialer and local emergency services. The project cannot dispatch help, identify your location, contact responders, send messages, or guarantee that a helpline is reachable at a specific moment.

## Calling Behavior

- Web call buttons use `tel:112` and `tel:181`.
- Android call buttons open the system dialer.
- The Android app does not request call permission and does not place calls automatically.
- The project does not auto-SMS trusted contacts.

## Safer Use

- Call only when it is safe to do so.
- If speaking may increase risk, consider moving to a safer location first if possible.
- Review the number shown in the dialer before placing the call.
- Keep trusted contacts saved directly on your device.
- Do not rely on this project as the only safety plan.

## Data Limits

Only two helplines are currently included because they have verified public sources. Missing local or state services may exist, but they are not included until they meet the verification policy.
