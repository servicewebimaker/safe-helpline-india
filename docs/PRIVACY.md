# Privacy

Safe Helpline India is designed to avoid collecting sensitive information.

## Web App

- No accounts.
- No cookies.
- No analytics.
- No forms for personal data.
- No contact collection.
- No location collection.
- Click-to-call links use `tel:` and rely on the user's device/browser.

## Widget

- No external dependencies.
- No cookies.
- No local storage.
- No network requests from the widget itself.
- No tracking.
- No contact or location collection.

## Android App

- No location permission.
- No contacts permission.
- No SMS permission.
- No camera or microphone permission.
- No storage permission.
- Helpline data is bundled for offline use.
- Tapping a call button opens the dialer with the number. The app does not place calls automatically.
- Tapping a source opens the user's browser through a standard Android intent.

## Trusted Contacts

The project encourages people to keep trusted contacts on their own device and make safety plans outside the app. The web app and Android app do not collect or store trusted contact details.
