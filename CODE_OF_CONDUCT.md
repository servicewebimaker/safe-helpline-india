# Code of Conduct

Safe Helpline India welcomes respectful, careful collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and professional.
- Assume good intent, while still reviewing safety-sensitive changes carefully.
- Use inclusive language.
- Respect privacy and personal boundaries.
- Ground public safety claims in verifiable sources.
- Accept maintainer decisions about data quality and user safety.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information.
- Submitting fabricated, unsourced, or misleading safety data.
- Pressuring maintainers to include unverified helplines.
- Adding tracking or intrusive permissions without clear necessity and review.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject pull requests, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project or its users.

If you need to report conduct concerns, contact the repository owner through a private channel listed on the repository profile after publication.
